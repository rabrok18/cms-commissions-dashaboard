How payouts work
  1. Commission is earned when an invoice is issued
  2. At month end, review each rep's total and click Record Payout
  3. Lump sum is saved with date and invoice list
  4. Invoices unpaid 90+ days are flagged for clawback
  5. Void a payout if recorded in error
Clawback: When a payout includes invoices later flagged for clawback, the at-risk amount is shown in history.
